About the artist

Johannes Vermeer, a Dutch Baroque painter, is celebrated for his masterful use of light and meticulous detail in his domestic interior scenes.

Historical context

The Dutch Golden Age, during the 17th century, was a period of great wealth and cultural achievement in the Netherlands, fostering the creation of timeless masterpieces.

Art movement

The Rococo art movement of the 18th century was characterized by ornate detail, light-hearted themes, and an emphasis on elegance and playfulness.
